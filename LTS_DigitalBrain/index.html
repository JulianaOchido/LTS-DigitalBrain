WHAT

The term "Digital Brain" describes an innovative approach to advancing technology so that it further resembles the human brain. This project aims to create the finest possible version of intelligence by identifying the weaknesses in our brains and applying them to the "Digital Brain."

ABOUT ITS PURPOSES.

Initial Thoughts

Initially, my thoughts are that this should bring simulations close to how the brain works and it will innovate the understanding human cognition. This will be done by getting deeper understanding of the human brain and develop Artificial Intelligence(AI).

Fears

To the moment, there are no fears from my side. The advancement of technological advances aimed at simulating the human brain may be the focus of this project. I suppose that in this instance, getting the results as exact as possible is the best approach to use.

Hopes

I have significant hopes for this project, expecting to contribute with my all to the path of this project. My expectations to it is that it involves human-machine interaction, exploring various ways of improving the behaviour of technologies everyday.
